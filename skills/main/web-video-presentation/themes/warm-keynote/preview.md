## Visual Snapshot
A cream paper backdrop is overlaid with a 40px warm-toned grid — soft beige lines that structure the space without feeling cold or technical. A large glass-morphism slab floats in the center, its 32px rounded corners and subtle backdrop-blur creating a frosted-glass card that hovers above the grid. Inter headlines sit clean and modern, while chunky black hero numerals in Fraunces anchor the key statistic. Teal accent appears as a single rule or highlight, never shouting — the visual language of a polished SaaS keynote that wants you to feel welcomed, not pitched.
