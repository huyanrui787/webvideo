## Visual Snapshot
A porcelain-white page holds text in deep indigo — not a navy, not a blue, but the specific depth of Chinese ink on handmade paper. Playfair Display italic headlines sit with scholarly composure, while Noto Serif SC renders Chinese characters with precise, even strokes that feel both contemporary and timeless. IBM Plex Sans carries any Latin body text with clarity. There is no ornament, no decoration, no flourish — the indigo ink against the pure white surface is the entire aesthetic. The composition feels like the opening page of an academic journal from a university press: rigorous, beautiful, and completely self-assured.
