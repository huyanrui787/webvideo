## Visual Snapshot
A warm peach-pink paper fills the frame, its surface carrying a coarse risograph-style grain that feels photocopied and slightly imperfect. Fraunces headlines sit chunky and confident, their serifs thick and friendly, while a dashed cut-line runs along one edge suggesting a zine page ready to be trimmed. Offset peach-colored shadows sit behind key letterforms, slightly misregistered like a riso drum that's drifted, and the magenta accent appears in small doses — a rule, a price-circle graphic, a hand-drawn star — never polished, always feeling like it was made last night at the copy shop.
