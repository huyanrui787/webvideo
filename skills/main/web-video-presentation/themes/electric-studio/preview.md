## Visual Snapshot
A pure white stage holds a single declaration in Manrope — geometric, modern, and utterly unadorned. The only chromatic voice is a 4px electric-blue bar pinned to the bottom edge of the title block, a deliberate signal of precision engineering rather than decoration. The dark shell bleeds in from the outer frame to bracket the white canvas, creating a confident corporate framing that never tips into coldness. Every element is measured: generous but not wasteful whitespace, a single sans-serif family run at scale, and that one electric-blue pulse that says enterprise without shouting.
