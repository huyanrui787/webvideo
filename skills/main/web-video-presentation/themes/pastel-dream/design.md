## Design Philosophy
Pastel Dream is built for friendliness without saccharine — a soft, welcoming visual language that feels professional enough for product onboarding and gentle enough for wellness content. The stage is a pastel blue canvas (#c8d9e6) with crisp white cards floating on generous rounded corners (20px). Sage green (#5a7c6a) serves as the accent — grounded and natural, avoiding the cloying quality of pink or lavender accents. The signature decorative element is a multi-color pill ribbon along one edge: a row of small rounded pills in pastel tones (sage, lavender, peach, sky, butter) that adds warmth and play without overwhelming the content. Plus Jakarta Sans carries the entire typographic system with its friendly geometric curves and open apertures. This theme is for product onboarding, friendly tutorials, wellness/mental health content, and approachable brand films. It is NOT for hardcore tech, investor pitches, legal/compliance, or cyberpunk aesthetics.

## Colors
The palette is soft pastels anchored by a grounded sage green accent. Shell (#c8d9e6) is pastel blue — a cool, calming stage floor that recedes gently. Surface (#faf9f7) is warm white — cards live here, crisp and clean against the pastel stage. Surface-2 (#eef0ee) provides a subtle tertiary plane. Text (#1f2429) is charcoal — softer than black, friendly but still professional. Text-2 (#3a4048) is lighter charcoal for secondary copy. Text-mute (#7a8088) and text-faint (#aab0b8) descend through neutral grays. The accent (#5a7c6a) is sage green — grounded, natural, and calming. It touches hero numbers, section markers, and the first pill in the ribbon. The multi-color pill ribbon introduces additional pastel tones (lavender #b8b8e8, peach #f0c8b0, sky #a8d8e8, butter #f0e8a0) — these are decorative only and never used for functional elements. The rule (#d8e0e8) is a soft pastel blue line — gentle and structural.

## Typography
Plus Jakarta Sans at 700 weight carries the voice — a friendly geometric sans-serif with open apertures, rounded terminals, and a welcoming presence that never feels childish. Plus Jakarta Sans at 400 weight handles body copy — the same typeface at different weights creates a cohesive, single-voice system. The geometric warmth carries through all sizes. JetBrains Mono at 500 serves code and technical content. CJK uses Noto Sans SC at 700 for display, matching Plus Jakarta Sans's friendly geometric character. The single-typeface (with CJK and mono additions) approach is intentional — Pastel Dream achieves its friendly coherence through typographic unity, not contrast.

## Layout & Components
The stage is pastel blue with a multi-color pill ribbon along one edge (typically left edge, occasionally bottom) — a row of small rounded pills (24px height, 12px border-radius, 16px gap) in pastel tones. The pill ribbon is the signature decoration and should appear on most slides (position may shift but color sequence should remain consistent). Cards are warm white (#faf9f7) with large rounded corners (20px border-radius) and soft, springy shadows — friendly and elevated without feeling heavy. Rules are 1px soft pastel blue lines — gentle structural hints. Hero numbers are Plus Jakarta Sans at 700 in sage green, tabular figures, 72-96px — friendly confidence. Density is medium — room for friendly layouts with supporting graphics. Animations should feel springy and organic.

## Do's
1. Apply the multi-color pill ribbon on most slides — it is the signature decorative element.
2. Use large rounded corners (20px) on all cards — the roundness is fundamental to the friendly character.
3. Use Plus Jakarta Sans for all text (display and body) — typographic unity creates coherence.
4. Keep the accent sage green usage restrained — hero numbers and section markers only.
5. Maintain generous padding (64px+ horizontal) — friendly design needs breathing room.
6. Use springy, organic animation curves — mechanical easing contradicts the soft character.
7. Keep the pill ribbon color sequence consistent across the deck — it is a brand element.

## Don'ts
1. Never use sharp corners (0 border-radius) — minimum 8px rounding everywhere, 20px on cards.
2. Never introduce dark, saturated, or aggressive colors — the palette is soft pastels only.
3. Never omit the pill ribbon on more than 2 consecutive slides — it is the theme's visual anchor.
4. Never use the pill ribbon colors for functional elements — they are decorative only.
5. Never fill a card with the sage green accent — it is for type only.
6. Never use serif typefaces — the sans-serif unity is essential to the friendly character.
7. Never use the sage green for body text — it is an accent, not an ink color.

## CJK Notes
Noto Sans SC at 700 weight for display and 400 weight for body matches Plus Jakarta Sans's friendly geometric character. CJK line-height minimum 1.75. The pill ribbon works identically across scripts — it is a color element, not a typographic one. CJK has no italic; use sage green accent for emphasis.
