## Visual Snapshot
A soft pastel blue-green washes across the backdrop, with a rounded cream card floating in the center like a gentle invitation. Plus Jakarta Sans renders the title in a friendly weight — clean and modern but without any corporate stiffness. A single-side multicolor pill strip runs vertically along one edge, its sage-green and soft-coral segments adding just enough playful color. All corners carry a generous 20px radius, softening every rectangle into something approachable, while the generous padding between elements gives the composition an airy, unhurried rhythm.
