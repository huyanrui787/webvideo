## Visual Snapshot
A deep navy-black field hums with a faint cyan grid, as if the slide itself were rendered inside a terminal from 2086. The title burns in Clash Display, haloed by a dual-neon text-shadow — cyan core with magenta bleed — that makes every letter feel electrically live. Satoshi handles the supporting type with sharp, future-facing geometry. The composition is saturated and unapologetically synthetic: glowing cyan rule lines, magenta accent strokes, and the constant suggestion of data flowing just beneath the surface. It is the visual language of a neural network booting up.
