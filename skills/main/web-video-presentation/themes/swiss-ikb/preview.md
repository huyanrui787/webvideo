Inter headlines sit in ultra-thin 200 weight, their letterforms so light they feel etched rather than printed, while IKB Klein Blue appears as the sole chromatic voice: a rule, a data point, a single numeral. Every element aligns to the modular grid with Swiss precision. Zero border-radius. The composition feels like a mid-century poster designed by someone who believes information IS beauty.
