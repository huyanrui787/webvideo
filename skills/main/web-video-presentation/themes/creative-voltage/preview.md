## Visual Snapshot
A saturated electric-blue field pulses beneath a halftone dot pattern, the CMYK-style rosettes scaled large enough to feel like pop-art texture rather than print artifact. Syne's wide geometric letterforms anchor the title, their proportions deliberately stretched and confident, while Space Mono handles supporting data with a rigid, machine-like counterpoint. Neon-yellow offset shadows sit behind key numerals and callouts, creating a chromatic misregistration effect that feels like a screen-printed gig poster pulled slightly off-register.
