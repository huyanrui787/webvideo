## Visual Snapshot
An ivory-cream page glows with the warmth of old paper, and the text itself is rendered in deep forest green — not a colorful accent, but the actual body ink. Playfair Display headlines carry that green with elegant serifs, their letterforms recalling the masthead of a mid-century nature magazine. Source Serif continues the green in running text, set in generous columns with comfortable leading. The only decoration is a thin botanical-green rule beneath the title and maybe a small leaf-like ornament, but the green ink IS the statement — no neon flourishes, just the quiet authority of a well-loved field journal.
