## Visual Snapshot
A pristine off-white canvas holds massive 900-weight hero numerals in Archivo Black, their edges razor-sharp with zero radius. A 4px solid black frame encloses the composition, while offset solid-color shadows in primary blue jut out from key forms. The geometry is unapologetic — thick horizontal rules, chunky rectangles, and a deliberate absence of curves or gradients create a manifesto-like graphic presence.
