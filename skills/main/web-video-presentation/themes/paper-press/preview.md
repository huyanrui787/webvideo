## Visual Snapshot
A warm cream page holds Instrument Serif italic headlines, their sharp editorial serifs and confident italic angle feeling pulled straight from a Sunday magazine supplement. The paper texture is subtle — a multiply-blended grain that softens the digital canvas into something tactile and approachable. A single hot orange-red accent punctuates the composition: a rule, a numeral, a small graphic marker — never more than one element at a time. IBM Plex Sans carries body text in a clean, neutral tone, letting the serif headlines and the warm paper do the expressive work.
