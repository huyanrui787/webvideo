## Visual Snapshot
Against a near-black backdrop, a single blurred pool of warm light glows softly — a vignette of amber and terracotta bleeding into the darkness, like lamplight through frosted glass. Cormorant italic headlines sit centered in the luminous zone, their high-contrast serifs and delicate hairlines exuding old-world editorial refinement. IBM Plex Sans carries body text in a restrained, narrow measure at the bottom, barely lit. The composition breathes: generous negative space and a single luminous focal point make the slide feel like the opening spread of a luxury monograph.
