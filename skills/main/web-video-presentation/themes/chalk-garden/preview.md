## Visual Snapshot

Every word appears in Patrick Hand, the handwritten letterforms slightly irregular and warmly imperfect, as if just scribbled with a piece of chalk. Dashed 2px chalk-yellow rules underline key phrases and separate sections, while small chalk-dot bullet points cluster beside supporting text. The overall feeling is a well-loved classroom blackboard at the end of a good lecture.
