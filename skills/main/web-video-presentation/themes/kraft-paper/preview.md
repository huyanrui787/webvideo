## Visual Snapshot
A textured kraft-paper beige surface fills the slide, its warmth suggesting recycled fibers and hand-pressed pulp. Fraunces headlines sit in deep brown ink, their organic serifs and slightly uneven weight distribution feeling hand-stamped rather than digitally set. Source Serif body text continues in the same brown, with comfortable leading and generous margins that recall a letterpress broadside. The texture is key — a subtle paper grain multiplied over the surface, making the digital frame feel like a physical object you could hold.
