## Visual Snapshot
A warm cream editorial page holds Fraunces italic headlines in a chunky, confident weight — the serifs thick and the italic angle forward-leaning, like a columnist leaning into a provocative take. Thin geometric overlay lines — circles, lines, and dots — form a delicate abstract constellation behind the title, adding visual wit without clutter. Work Sans carries body text in a conversational tone, the line length kept short for punchy readability. A warm terracotta accent appears sparingly: one rule, one numeral, one small decorative dot — the visual equivalent of a raised eyebrow.
