## Visual Snapshot
A dark stage swept by a subtle diagonal gradient from near-black to charcoal, punctuated by a single oversized hot-orange focal card that commands the frame. Archivo Black headlines hit at maximum scale with tight tracking — urgent, confident, one-statement-per-slide. The only light in the system is the orange signal: a luminous card, a glowing hero numeral, a thin accent rule slicing the dark.
