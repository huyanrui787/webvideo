## Visual Snapshot
The screen splits cleanly down the middle — warm peach on the left, cool lavender on the right — a hard 50/50 division with no gradient or softening. Outfit's rounded geometric letterforms span across the divide, the text colored to contrast against each half. The split itself is the design: no other major decorative elements compete with the fundamental two-tone structure. Small graphic marks or numerals may sit on one side or the other, but the primary gesture is the canvas itself divided into two distinct color zones, each ready to carry its own idea or counterpoint.
